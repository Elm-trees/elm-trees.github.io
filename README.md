Hi! My name is Eli "Elm" Markert. I am currently a PhD Student at Boston University where I am a fellow in BU's NRT on Biological Feedback Control. I conduct research in the [Dunlop Lab](https://www.dunloplab.com/). My focus is on stochastic gene expression through the lens of uncertainty quantification via deep learning. My primary background is in Statistical & Data Sciences, but I have been doing computational biology research since my first year of college. I am passionate about storytelling, scientifically and otherwise. Outside of science, I enjoy reading, writing, and birding among other things.
